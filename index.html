Asha Richardson is an Oakland digital native, who loves everything art, tech, and business. Asha graduated from Mills College Spring 2013, with a B.A. in Economics and minor in Ethnic Studies. While in college, Asha cofounded Youth Radio's newest department, the App Lab. The Lab partners young people with professional developers and designers to create apps, that engage the community. The App Lab, is now a part of the Innovation Lab, bringing tech,data, and engagement to Youth Radio's award winning newsroom.
